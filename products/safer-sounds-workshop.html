What's included

  • A 60-minute guided somatic experience Not a lecture — an experience. You'll slow down a real trigger moment and understand your reaction from the inside. Work through it with any trigger, as many times as you'd like.
  • A guided mindfulness & somatic regulation practice A body-based practice to return to anytime: before a triggering environment, after a reaction hits, or on the days that things feel like too much.
  • The Misophonia Relief Map worksheet Your own personal map of what's underneath your reaction — the sensations, emotions, beliefs, and unmet needs your nervous system has been reaching for all along. Bring it to your therapist or work through it on your own. Specific to your nervous system, your pattern, your history.

You're ready for this if…

  • You've eaten alone more times than you want to admit.
  • You've left meals early, turned down trips, and made your world smaller to avoid triggering sounds.
  • You know exactly who the gum chewers are in your life and you've never figured out how to talk to them about it.
  • You've been told you're too sensitive — often by the same people whose sounds bother you most.
  • You've tried the coping strategies. They helped a little. You're still here.
  • You want to sit at the dinner table. Sleep in your own bed. Say yes to things again.
